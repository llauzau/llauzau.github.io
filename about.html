Hi, I'm Lauren!

I'm a designer, font-end developer, and mom of three.

I believe that good design can be both functional and beautiful. I think designers have the power to push for new ideas and should always be an advocate for the user.

I started my career as a full-time print designer, but kept finding myself gravitating toward projects that were multi-media. I enjoy trying to figure out how things work and solving complex problems. I've spent the last 3.5 years figuring out how to not only create nice UX/UI experiences, but also how to make them work.

As a mom of three, life is never dull. I enjoy spending time with my family and try to get outside as often as possible. When I'm not working, I can often be found gardening, hiking or biking or traveling to one of the coutries National Parks.
